Zaak Afhandel App

Zaak Afhandel App brings ZGW-aligned case handling into Nextcloud: intake to closure for zaken, defined status workflows, the work queue, tasks for staff and citizens, attached documents, and a record of every decision (besluit) — no separate case system, no second login.
